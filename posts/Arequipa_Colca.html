Arequipa & Colca Canyon

Jul 25 2016

The two deepest canyons on earth are both in Peru: Cotahuasi (11,001 ft) and Colca (10,040 ft), which is accesible from the city of Arequipa (nicknamed 'La Ciudad Blanca' sitting between 4 volcanoes). I spent an afternoon in Arequipa admiring the churches and the Monasterio de Santa Catalina (a convent dating back to 1579) and had the best ceviche of my trip at Cevicher√≠a Fory Fay.


The next day I departed on a two day hike which I arranged through my hostel and descended into the canyon after stopping at the Cruz del Condor to admire the legendary birds. Hiking Sticks again proved supreme and the variation in climate signature to Peru presented itself. We woke before sunrise the following morning to pass the 3 hour hike up the canyon in the shade.
