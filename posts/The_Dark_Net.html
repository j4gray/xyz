The Dark Net by Jaime

Apr 7 2017

Whether you're interested in utilizing the services of the dark net or not, it is a fascinating system that offers a myriad of different tools and services. The topic became part of public discourse with the rise and fall of Silk Road, a blackmarket that operated solely on the internet. The underlying system that enabled Silk Road is the dark web accessed via software called Tor or "The Onion Router" and allows for annonymous use of the Internet. Bartlett's book gives a tour of the dark web and its available services as well as insight into the people who built the corresponding software (including the minds behind Bitcoin) and those who utilize it.
