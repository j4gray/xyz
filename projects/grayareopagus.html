Gray Areopagus


I made this website to practice writing and implementing simple javascript functions on websites. I had the idea after participating in a 'Bad Hacks' Hackathon and creating a calculator that displays results in various exotic numerical systems (including binary and Egyptian hieroglyphics via emojis) using html and javascript. I tried to create applications for the web that I myself would use. I started by posting articles that redefined the way I see contemporary scientific and technological advancment (particularly in the fields of neuroscience, Artificial Inteligence and space exploration). To make them a little more visually appealing I used Flash to animate my Bitmoji and added that to the pages. Then as I learned javascript I implemented simple arrays to create apps for two of my favorite hobbies: Wikipedia and Crossword puzzles. Finally, I used what I had learned using a game engine in Racket during 'Fundamentals of Computer Programming' to create my favorite game of all time, what my mother affectionately called 'Waste your Time' when she would steal my GameBoy Color to play: Tetris... with a twist. It's designed to be used as a study aid so everytime you get a line you are flashed a trivia question.


The name is a portmanteau of 'gray area' and 'Areopagus,' which was an ancient Athenian court mentioned in Euripides' Orestes. The resulting combination means an attempt to analyze and judge things that are ambiguous. Here's a catalog of Gray Areopagus:


  1. Artificial Intelligence - what is the current landscape of AI and what are some projections from the future (current research + sci fi)
  2. Space - The landscape of our solar system and the Fermi paradox
  3. Neuroscience - Amazing articles from an assortment of visionaries
  4. Wikipedia - Surf through some of my favorite Wikipedia pages
  5. Calculator - Do your everyday arithmatic using the Mayan system of human apendages
  6. Crosswords - Try to solve some of my original crosswords (best way to improve your memory)
  7. Trivia Tetris - Study for a big test WHILE wasting your time
  8. About - email me at julia@grayareopagus.com
