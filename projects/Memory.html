The name is a portmanteau of 'gray area' and 'Areopagus,' which was an ancient Athenian court mentioned in Euripides' Orestes. The resulting combination means an attempt to analyze and judge things that are ambiguous. Here's a catalog of Gray Areopagus:


  1. Artificial Intelligence - what is the current landscape of AI and what are some projections from the future (current research + sci fi)
  2. Space - The landscape of our solar system and the Fermi paradox
  3. Neuroscience - Amazing articles from an assortment of visionaries
  4. Wikipedia - Surf through some of my favorite Wikipedia pages
  5. Calculator - Do your everyday arithmatic using the Mayan system of human apendages
  6. Crosswords - Try to solve some of my original crosswords (best way to improve your memory)
  7. Trivia Tetris - Study for a big test WHILE wasting your time
  8. About - email me at julia@grayareopagus.com
