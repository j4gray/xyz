I created this site using Sublime to house my resume and as an opportunity to master HTML and CSS. The photos were taken by me or one of my travel companions, including the incredibly talented Steven Sachs. I love Wikipedia and shamelessly cite it in my blog posts because people who say it is not a real source are wrong, it is a collective representation of human understanding at any given moment in time on any given issue.
